    Footprint Chart

    Footprint Chart helps you to understand the present market conditions from multiple perspectives -Price, Volume, Imbalance, Point of Control (POC), Buy&Sell Pressure, but also volume and a dynamic color which signifies more aggressive buying or selling, this is the result of whether more trading occurred on the bid or ask. Footprint Charts provide better understanding because you can see everything in a single bar.

        + Profitable in M3 time frame
        + See inside the bar
        + Responsive to market conditions
        + Works off the "hard ride edge" of a chart

    DELTA

    The Volume Delta (Vol ∆) study displays the total amount of transactions occurring on both the Bid and the Ask in a given interval, but also superimposes the absolute value of the net difference between the BVol and AVol for that interval. Whichever is greater (BVol or AVol) is displayed in that color on top of the total volume.

        + Profitable in M3 time frame
        + See the net volume in the bar
        + Buy&Sell Pressure

    MARKET PROFILE

    Let’s look at Volume at Price before we explain these important daily price levels. Volume at Price is generally plotted on a chart to view which price levels have attracted high-volume trading and which price levels have relatively low volumes. These high- and low-volume areas often form bell-shaped curves turned on their side, and are commonly referred to as volume profiles.
